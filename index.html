About Me

I am a BS Information Technology graduate specializing in Software Development, focused on building practical full-stack and mobile systems. My work spans Laravel, NestJS, Flutter, Vue, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Firebase, and workflow-heavy applications for agriculture, government inventory, HR, payroll, marketplaces, and internal operations. I like turning real process problems into usable software with clear roles, reliable data, and interfaces people can actually work with.


My journey in web development began with a curiosity to understand the intricacies of building for the web. I have been immersed in learning and exploring various technologies, from core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to modern frameworks and backend tools. The dynamic nature of programming and the ever-evolving tech landscape fuel my constant desire to enhance my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the field.
